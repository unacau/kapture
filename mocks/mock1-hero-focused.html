Why Kapture?

Browser automation is essential for AI agents, but existing solutions struggle with:

  • Unreliable clicking and element interaction - Traditional tools frequently fail to click elements or interact with modern web applications
  • Complex setup and dependencies - Most solutions require Selenium, Chrome drivers, or headless browser configurations
  • Poor error handling - Cryptic errors that are difficult for AI agents to understand and recover from
  • Connection instability - WebSocket disconnections and lack of reconnection support
  • Limited debugging capabilities - No easy way to test commands or see what's happening

Kapture solves these problems with a fresh approach built specifically for AI-driven automation.
